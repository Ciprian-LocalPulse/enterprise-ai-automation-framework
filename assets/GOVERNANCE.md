## Decision-Making Framework

### Tier 1 — Sole Authority (Ciprian Stefan Plesca)
Decisions made exclusively by the governing authority, not subject to community input:

- Content direction and major module changes
- Licensing terms and access authorization
- Security policy and CISO-level architecture decisions
- Pricing and commercial positioning
- Exit or transfer of the blueprint or business
- Enforcement of the Code of Conduct
- Legal matters and IP disputes

### Tier 2 — Advisory Input (Authorized Partners)
Licensed enterprise partners may provide advisory input — but not binding votes — on:

- Template improvements and additions
- New niche research and market intelligence
- Compliance framework updates for emerging regulations
- Community program enhancements

Advisory input is submitted via [CONTACT@LOCALPULSE.PRO](mailto:CONTACT@LOCALPULSE.PRO) and reviewed quarterly.

### Tier 3 — Community Feedback (Licensed Practitioners)
Licensed practitioners may submit feedback through:

- GitHub Issues (documentation errors, template bugs)
- Community forum threads (feature suggestions)
- Post-implementation surveys

Feedback is reviewed and incorporated at the governing authority's discretion.

## Conflict Resolution

In the event of a dispute between community members, licensed partners, or contributors:

1. **Direct resolution** — Parties attempt resolution between themselves.
2. **Escalation** — Dispute escalated to [CONTACT@LOCALPULSE.PRO](mailto:CONTACT@LOCALPULSE.PRO).
3. **Governing authority decision** — Ciprian Stefan Plesca issues a final binding decision.
4. **Legal** — If unresolved, proceedings under applicable law.

The governing authority's decision in Step 3 is final and not subject to further appeal within the community.
